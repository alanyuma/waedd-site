Comprehensive Economic Development Strategy (CEDS)

CEDS are locally based and regionally driven economic development planning processes.

These CEDS have long been a cornerstone of the US Economic Development Administration's programs and serve to engage community leaders, leverage involvement of the private sector and establish strategic blueprints for regional collaboration.

We currently manage the recently published 2020-2021 CEDS for our three-county region.
