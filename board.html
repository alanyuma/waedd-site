Board Meetings

Regular meetings of the District Organization shall be held at least twice per year and are open to the public.

Special meetings of the District Organization may be called by the Chairman or upon the request of a majority of regular members of the District Organization.

The Executive Committee shall meet at least four (4) times each year.
