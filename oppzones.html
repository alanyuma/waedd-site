Opportunity Zones

Opportunity Zones were created under the 2017 Tax Cuts and Jobs Act, signed into law by President Donald J. Trump on December 22, 2017, to stimulate economic development and job creation, by incentivizing long-term investments in low-income neighborhoods.

The initiative provides a unique investment vehicle to engage communities and regional leaders to promote and direct investment to and spur job creation in underserved, impoverished, or otherwise distressed communities throughout the country.
