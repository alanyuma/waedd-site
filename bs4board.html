Board of Directors - 2020

General Board of Directors meetings are conducted twice per calendar year on the last Thursday of March and September.

Executive Board and Management Committee meetings are conducted four times per calendar year on the last Thursday of January, April, June, and October.

Board meeting locations rotate alphabetically to each county.

Board meetings are always open to the public and are accessible.

Remote meeting participation is always available.

The next scheduled General Board of Directors Meeting is Thursday, September 24, 2020, at 5:30 pm at the District office in Kingman Arizona.

The next scheduled Executive Board and Management Committee Meeting is Thursday, October 29, 2020, at 5:30 pm in La Paz County.
